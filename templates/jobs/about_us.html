About Us

We empower African developers.

We help build technology careers in Africa

Techonect Africa is the name of the most reliable site for graduates in fields of STEM(Science,Technology,Engineering and Maths) and selftaught software engineers that are looking for tech careers in Africa and employers looking for new hires. Our platform is available for anyone looking to work in any Technology company within Africa. The jobs posted here are realistic and actually do exist. Our goal is to link reliable employers with the best candidates available, to create technology careers in Africa.

Our Team

Our team exists of highly passionate people that worked part of their careers in Africa and have a specific bond with the African continent. Unlike most job search sites we feel that everybody that took the time to contact us deserves a response. Therefore we have developed a very personal and informal way of working. With this approach we have realised many successful matches in the past and will continue to increase this number in the coming years!
